## Human Choice, Heaven's Selection: On People | 人选天选人

> 主要是描述如何通过学习人们选择中贪婪和代价的符号，建立天机，概率性的判断和预测一个人和事物的内心世界，但代价巨大。通过天机观测天权，你会发现天权在对抗和分裂统一的循环态往复，而我们希望改变环境和结构的真正机会，就是天权统一和天权分裂的关键节点，这时人选天选的反馈效率特别高。在天权结构稳定的时候，没人能通过自己改变结构，无论你多努力反馈效率都很低。但切记：反馈效率的提高和我们期待的机会本身，只能用于建立更加稳定的结构、调节天权，否则会在结果中付出巨大的代价。

This volume mainly describes how to build Celestial Insight by studying the symbols of Greed and Cost in the choices people make — enabling probabilistic judgment and prediction of a person's or a situation's inner world, though at great cost. By observing Celestial Balance through the lens of Celestial Insight, you will find that Celestial Balance cycles endlessly through states of opposition, division, and unification. The real opportunities for us to change our environment and its structures arise at the critical junctures where Celestial Balance unifies or fractures — at these moments, the feedback efficiency of Human Choice and Heaven's Selection is exceptionally high. When the structure of Celestial Balance is stable, no one can alter it through individual effort alone; no matter how hard you try, the feedback efficiency remains low. But remember this: the heightened feedback efficiency and the opportunities we long for must only be used to build more stable structures and to regulate Celestial Balance — otherwise, the Cost exacted in the outcome will be enormous.
