# Aside

## A Philosophy of Choice and Cost

The complete original text of this book will be published online. It primarily expounds the author's personal cosmology, worldview, and values.

## Human Choice, Celestial Selection: On People

This volume mainly describes how to build Celestial Insight by studying the symbols of Greed and Cost in the choices people make — enabling probabilistic judgment and prediction of a person's or a situation's inner world, though at great cost. By observing Celestial Balance through the lens of Celestial Insight, you will find that Celestial Balance cycles endlessly through states of opposition, division, and unification. The real opportunities for us to change our environment and its structures arise at the critical junctures where Celestial Balance unifies or fractures — at these moments, the feedback efficiency of human choice and celestial selection is exceptionally high. When the structure of Celestial Balance is stable, no one can alter it through individual effort alone; no matter how hard you try, the feedback efficiency remains low. But remember this: the heightened feedback efficiency and the opportunities we long for must only be used to build more stable structures and to regulate Celestial Balance — otherwise, the Cost exacted in the outcome will be enormous.

## Human Choice, Celestial Selection: On Heaven

This volume mainly describes the contemplation of seeking out causal ledgers and observer deception, the exploration of paths toward Double Forgetting, and the idealist hypothesis of glimpsing beyond the veil.
